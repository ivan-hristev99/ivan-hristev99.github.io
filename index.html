Accommodation

We suggest you arrive in Dorset the day before the wedding. We will help organise transport from your hotel to the wedding venue in the morning and back to your hotel in the evening. They are many local hotel options, including:

The Ollerod Hotel

Dating from the 13th Century, The Ollerod is a magical Dorset country house hotel full of character - the product of generations of history passing through its doors. The Ollerod offers 13 individually styled hotel bedrooms, including 2 dog-friendly bedrooms.
Distance from Mapperton: 2 miles
The Ollerod

Laverstock Farm

Laverstock Farm can be found at the end of long drive, located deep in a lost coombe in breath-taking West Dorset. It comprises six holiday cottages of different sizes and two modern shepherd's huts all set on a working farm near Bridport. Laverstock Farm has accommodation to sleep up to a total of 30 people.
Distance from Mapperton: 8 miles
Laverstock Farm

North End Farm

North End Farm dates from before 1600 with some parts reconstructed after a fire in 1640. Features such as an Inglenook fireplace, old bread oven and mullion windows combined with plenty of outdoor space. North End Farmhouse provides accommodation for up to 14 people in 6 bedrooms.
Distance from Mapperton: 11 miles
North End Farm

The Seaside Boarding House

The Seaside Boarding House is a unique collaboration of old and new; lovingly renovated furniture and fittings from the 1920s to 1980's, marine salvage, nautical paintings and then touches like a fabulous collection of Peter Blake prints on loan from art dealer Paul Stolper, that fit perfectly with the ageless decor. The Seaside Boarding House offers 9 bedrooms.
Distance from Mapperton: 8.5 miles
Seaside Boarding House

Symondsbury Manor

Behind Symondsbury Manor's mellow stone walls and mullioned windows lie a surprising discovery: a fresh, creative interior full of colour and playful charm. Symondsbury Manor offers 11 bedrooms.
Distance from Mapperton: 8 miles
Symondsbury Manor

How do I get there?

It's way easier than you think!

By Road

Mapperton is located in Beaminster, Dorset. From Beaminster, take the B3163. From Crewkerne go via the A303 and A3066 and from Dorchester go via the A37 and A356. If you're using a sat nav, the postcode is DT8 3NR and there's a car park on-site. Travel time from London is around 3 hours.

By Rail

The closest train stations are Crewkerne (15 minutes away) and Dorchester (25 minutes away), with direct links to London.
